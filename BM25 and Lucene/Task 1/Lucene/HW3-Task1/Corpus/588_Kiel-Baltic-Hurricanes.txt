www.baltic-hurricanes.de the kiel baltic hurricanes are an american football team based in kiel germany the club has been of recent a strong force in the sport in germany playing in five consecutive german bowls from 2008 to 2012 losing the first two winning the third and losing the fourth and fifth the club is part of the american sports club kiel e.v 1 history edit hurricanes offensive tackle
