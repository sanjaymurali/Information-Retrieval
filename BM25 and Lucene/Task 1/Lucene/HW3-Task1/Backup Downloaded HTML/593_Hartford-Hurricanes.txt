<p>The <b>Hartford Hurricanes</b> were an American <a href="/wiki/Basketball" title="Basketball">basketball</a> team based in <a href="/wiki/Hartford,_Connecticut" title="Hartford, Connecticut">Hartford, Connecticut</a> that was a member of the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/American_Basketball_League_(1925-1955)" title="American Basketball League (1925-1955)">American Basketball League</a>.</p>
<p>The team was previously known as the <a href="/wiki/Elizabeth_Braves" title="Elizabeth Braves">Elizabeth Braves</a> who moved to Hartford during the 1947/48 season on December 19, 1947.</p>
