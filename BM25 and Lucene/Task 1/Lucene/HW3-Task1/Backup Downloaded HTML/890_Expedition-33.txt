<p><b>Expedition 33</b> was the 33rd long-duration <a href="/wiki/List_of_International_Space_Station_expeditions" title="List of International Space Station expeditions">expedition</a> to the <a href="/wiki/International_Space_Station" title="International Space Station">International Space Station</a> (ISS). It began on 16 September 2012 with the departure from the ISS of the <a href="/wiki/Soyuz_TMA-04M" title="Soyuz TMA-04M">Soyuz TMA-04M</a> spacecraft, which returned the <a href="/wiki/Expedition_32" title="Expedition 32">Expedition 32</a> crew to Earth.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-begin_1-2"><a href="#cite_note-begin-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
