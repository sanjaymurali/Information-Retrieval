<h1 class="firstHeading" id="firstHeading" lang="en">Hurricane, Alabama</h1>
<div class="mw-body-content" id="bodyContent">

<div id="contentSub"></div>
<div class="mw-jump" id="jump-to-nav">

<div class="mw-content-ltr" dir="ltr" id="mw-content-text" lang="en"><div class="mw-parser-output"><p><b>Hurricane</b> is an <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Unincorporated_community" title="Unincorporated community">unincorporated community</a> in <a href="/wiki/Baldwin_County,_Alabama" title="Baldwin County, Alabama">Baldwin County, Alabama</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-GNIS_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-GNIS-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Hurricane,_Alabama&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Variant names were "Hurricane Bayou" and "Tensaw Station".<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-GNIS_1-1"><a href="#cite_note-GNIS-1">[1]</a></sup> A <a href="/wiki/Post_office" title="Post office">post office</a> called Hurricane Bayou was established in 1877, the name was changed to Hurricane in 1895, and the post office closed in 1962.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> The community was named for the fact a hurricane had struck the area.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup></p>
