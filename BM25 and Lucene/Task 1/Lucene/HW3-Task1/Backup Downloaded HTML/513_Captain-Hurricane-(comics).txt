<p><b>Captain Hurricane</b> was a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Fictional" title="Fictional">fictional</a> <a href="/wiki/Comic_book" title="Comic book">comic book</a> character in <a href="/wiki/Valiant_(comics)" title="Valiant (comics)">Valiant</a> during the 1960s and 1970s.</p>
<p>He was in the <a href="/wiki/United_Kingdom" title="United Kingdom">British</a> <a href="/wiki/3_Commando_Brigade" title="3 Commando Brigade">Royal Marines Commandos</a>, fighting against the <a href="/wiki/Germany" title="Germany">German</a> and <a href="/wiki/Japan" title="Japan">Japanese</a> <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Armed_forces" title="Armed forces">armed forces</a> in the <a href="/wiki/World_War_II" title="World War II">2nd World War</a>. With his <a href="/wiki/Chief_mate" title="Chief mate">first mate</a> and <a href="/wiki/Batman_(military)#United_Kingdom" title="Batman (military)">batman</a>, "Maggots" Malone, he battled the <a href="/wiki/Axis_powers" title="Axis powers">Axis powers</a> with his super human strength that appeared whenever he became enraged.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-nationalhero_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-nationalhero-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
