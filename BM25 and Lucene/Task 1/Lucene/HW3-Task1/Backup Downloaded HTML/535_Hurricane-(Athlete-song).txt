<p>"<b>Hurricane</b>" is a song by <a href="/wiki/England" title="England">English</a> <a href="/wiki/Indie_rock" title="Indie rock">indie</a> <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Rock_band" title="Rock band">rock band</a> <a href="/wiki/Athlete_(band)" title="Athlete (band)">Athlete</a> and is the second track on their 2007 album <i><a href="/wiki/Beyond_the_Neighbourhood" title="Beyond the Neighbourhood">Beyond the Neighbourhood</a></i>. The song was released as the first <a href="/wiki/Single_(music)" title="Single (music)">single</a> from that album on 27 August 2007. "Hurricane" did not receive as much success as their previous singles "Wires" and "Half Light" only reaching number 31 in the UK Charts. (see <a href="/wiki/2007_in_British_music" title="2007 in British music">2007 in British music</a>).<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-athlete_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-athlete-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
