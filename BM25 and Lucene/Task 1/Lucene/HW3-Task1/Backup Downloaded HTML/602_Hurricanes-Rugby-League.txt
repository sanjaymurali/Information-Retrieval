<p><b>Hurricanes Rugby League</b> are a <a href="/wiki/Jamaica" title="Jamaica">Jamaican</a> <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Rugby_league_football" title="Rugby league football">rugby league football</a> team.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> Formed in 2011 with the intention of becoming Jamaica's first <a href="/wiki/Professional_sports" title="Professional sports">professional</a> rugby league team, they hope to play in one of the domestic competitions in the <a href="/wiki/United_States" title="United States">United States</a> by 2013<sup class="noprint Inline-Template" style="margin-left:0.1em; white-space:nowrap;">[<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Manual_of_Style/Dates_and_numbers#Precise_language" title="Wikipedia:Manual of Style/Dates and numbers"><span title="The text near this tag is dated.">needs update</span></a></i>]</sup>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup></p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Hurricanes_Rugby_League&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The Hurricanes Rugby League were officially launched on the 26 February 2011 at GC Forster College, <a href="/wiki/Spanish_Town" title="Spanish Town">Spanish Town</a>, <a href="/wiki/Jamaica" title="Jamaica">Jamaica</a>. The Hurricanes' aim to create a professional rugby league team to compete in one of the domestic competitions in the United States, the <a href="/wiki/American_National_Rugby_League" title="American National Rugby League">American National Rugby League</a> (AMNRL) or the <a href="/wiki/USA_Rugby_League" title="USA Rugby League">USA Rugby League</a> (USARL) by 2013. The team was founded by Australian <a href="/wiki/Dane_Campbell" title="Dane Campbell">Dane Campbell</a>, a former player for the <a href="/wiki/Newcastle_Knights" title="Newcastle Knights">Newcastle Knights</a> of the <a href="/wiki/National_Rugby_League" title="National Rugby League">National Rugby League</a> (NRL). Campbell is the sole owner of the franchise.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup></p>
<p>In 2012 the Hurricanes hosted the <a href="/wiki/USA_Rugby_League" title="USA Rugby League">USA Rugby League</a> club the <a href="/wiki/Washington_D.C._Slayers" title="Washington D.C. Slayers">Washington D.C. Slayers</a> in <a href="/wiki/Kingston,_Jamaica" title="Kingston, Jamaica">Kingston</a> in the first ever international club match on Jamaican soil. The Hurricanes the preseason matchup 66-29.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-6"><a href="#cite_note-6">[6]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-7"><a href="#cite_note-7">[7]</a></sup></p>
