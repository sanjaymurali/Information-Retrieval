<p><b>Expedition 52</b> is the 52nd expedition to the <a href="/wiki/International_Space_Station" title="International Space Station">International Space Station</a>. It officially began on June 2, 2017 10:47 UTC, with the undocking of <a href="/wiki/Soyuz_MS-03" title="Soyuz MS-03">Soyuz MS-03</a>. Transfer of Command from <a href="/wiki/Expedition_51" title="Expedition 51">Expedition 51</a> was done on June 1<sup>st</sup>, 2017.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-start_1-1"><a href="#cite_note-start-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
<p>Due to a decision to cut down the number of participating Russian cosmonauts in 2017, only two crew members were launched on <a href="/wiki/Soyuz_MS-04" title="Soyuz MS-04">Soyuz MS-04</a>, which brought the ISS total crew down to five people.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> However, it was later decided that <a href="/wiki/Peggy_Whitson" title="Peggy Whitson">Peggy Whitson</a> would stay on board longer, transferring from Expedition 51 to maintain a full crew of six astronauts over the summer, after the arrival of three new members on <a href="/wiki/Soyuz_MS-05" title="Soyuz MS-05">Soyuz MS-05</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-whitson-summer_3-0"><a href="#cite_note-whitson-summer-3">[3]</a></sup></p>
