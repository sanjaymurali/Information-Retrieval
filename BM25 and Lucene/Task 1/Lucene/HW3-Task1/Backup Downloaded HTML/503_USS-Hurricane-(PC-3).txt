<p><b><a href="/wiki/United_States_Ship" title="United States Ship">USS</a> <i>Hurricane</i> (PC-3)</b> is the third <a href="/wiki/Cyclone-class_patrol_ship" title="Cyclone-class patrol ship"><i>Cyclone</i>-class patrol</a> (coastal) ship. Her keel was laid at <a href="/wiki/Bollinger_Shipyards" title="Bollinger Shipyards">Bollinger Shipyards</a> on 20 November 1991, and she was launched on 6 June 1992 and commissioned on 15 October 1993.</p>
<p>In 2012, <i>Hurricane</i> made port visits to various cities in the <a href="/wiki/Great_Lakes" title="Great Lakes">Great Lakes</a> region to commemorate the bicentennial of the <a href="/wiki/War_of_1812" title="War of 1812">War of 1812</a>.</p>
