<p>The <b>Harmarville Hurricanes Soccer Club</b> were an amateur <a href="/wiki/Association_football" title="Association football">soccer</a> club from <a href="/wiki/Harmarville,_Pennsylvania" title="Harmarville, Pennsylvania">Harmarville, Pennsylvania</a>, a <a href="/wiki/Suburb" title="Suburb">suburb</a> of <a href="/wiki/Pittsburgh" title="Pittsburgh">Pittsburgh</a>, that twice won the <a href="/wiki/Lamar_Hunt_U.S._Open_Cup" title="Lamar Hunt U.S. Open Cup">Lamar Hunt U.S. Open Cup</a>, known then as the National Challenge Cup, in <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/1952_U.S._Open_Cup" title="1952 U.S. Open Cup">1952</a> and <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/1956_U.S._Open_Cup" title="1956 U.S. Open Cup">1956</a>. They also played in the Cup Final in <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/1953_U.S._Open_Cup" title="1953 U.S. Open Cup">1953</a>. The 1956 Final saw the Hurricanes win 3–2 on <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Aggregate_score" title="Aggregate score">aggregate score</a> after a comeback from a two-goal deficit; they lost the first leg on the road 1–0 and conceded the first goal in the return home leg.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
