the united states on 5 march 2002 the name of this album comes from the location of imbruglia s home in windsor 1 on a peninsula of that name between the clewer mill stream and the main stream of the river thames singles edit that day was released on 29 october 2001 as the lead single of the album it peaked at number 11 at the uk singles chart 2 wrong impression was released on 29 january 2002 as the second single of the album it peaked at number 7 at the u.s billboard adult top 40 beauty on the fire was released on 22 july 2002 as the third and last single of the album it peaked at number 26
