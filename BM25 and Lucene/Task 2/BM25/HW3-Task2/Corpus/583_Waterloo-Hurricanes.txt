operated 1950  1950 –1952 home arena waterloo memorial arena the waterloo hurricanes were a junior ice hockey team playing in the ontario hockey association for two seasons between 1950 and 1952 they played at the waterloo memorial arena in waterloo ontario the team folded after the 1951–52 season due to financial woes that season the hurricanes competed against cross-town rivals the kitchener greenshirts two alumni of the hurricanes went on to play in the national hockey league goaltender norm defelice played 10 games with the boston bruins in 1956–57 and had a long career in the eastern
