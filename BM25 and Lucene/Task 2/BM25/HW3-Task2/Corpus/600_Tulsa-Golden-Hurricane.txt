tulsahurricane.com the golden hurricanes are the athletic teams that represent the university of tulsa these teams are referred to as the tulsa golden hurricane or variously as hurricane tu or tulsa before adopting the name golden hurricane in 1922 the university of tulsa tu had many unofficial team nicknames including kendallites from tu s predecessor institution henry kendall college presbyterians from the university s founding by the presbyterian church tulsans tigers orange and black and yellow jackets the name golden tornadoes was chosen by tu football coach h.m archer 1922–24 based on new gold and black uniforms rather than the previous orange and black and a remark made during practice of the team roaring through opponents during a season when tu went undefeated including wins over texas a m and the university of arkansas however it was quickly discovered that the same name had been chosen in 1917 by georgia tech archer then substituted the term hurricane for tornado
