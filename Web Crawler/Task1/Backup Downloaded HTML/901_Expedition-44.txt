<p><b>Expedition 44</b> was the 44th expedition to the <a href="/wiki/International_Space_Station" title="International Space Station">International Space Station</a>. It commenced with the departure of <a href="/wiki/Soyuz_TMA-15M" title="Soyuz TMA-15M">Soyuz TMA-15M</a> from the ISS with the <a href="/wiki/Expedition_42" title="Expedition 42">Expedition 42</a>/<a href="/wiki/Expedition_43" title="Expedition 43">43</a> crew on 11 June 2015, and ended with the departure of <a href="/wiki/Soyuz_TMA-16M" title="Soyuz TMA-16M">Soyuz TMA-16M</a> on 11 September 2015.</p>
<p><a href="/wiki/Yury_Lonchakov" title="Yury Lonchakov">Yury Lonchakov</a> was originally supposed to be the commander of Expedition 44 following being Flight Engineer 3 on <a href="/wiki/Expedition_43" title="Expedition 43">Expedition 43</a>. However, he resigned from the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Russian_Federal_Space_Agency" title="Russian Federal Space Agency">Russian Federal Space Agency</a> on September 6, 2013.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
