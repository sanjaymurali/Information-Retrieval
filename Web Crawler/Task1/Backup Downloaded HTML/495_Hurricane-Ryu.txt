<div class="mw-content-ltr" dir="ltr" id="mw-content-text" lang="en"><div class="mw-parser-output"><p><b>Hariken Ryu</b> <span style="font-weight: normal">(<span lang="ja" xml:lang="ja">破李拳竜</span><span style="display:none">,</span> <i>Hariken Ryu</i>, born January 22, 1958)</span>, nicknamed Hurricane Ryu, is a Japanese illustrator, comic book author, writer, director, martial artist and an <a href="/wiki/Actor" title="Actor">actor</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-kalat2007_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-kalat2007-1">[1]</a></sup> His real name is Hidemi Miyata, born in Tochigi Prefecture.</p>
