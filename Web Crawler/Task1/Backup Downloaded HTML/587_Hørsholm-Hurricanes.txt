<p><b>Hørsholm Hurricanes</b> is the oldest <a href="/wiki/Softball" title="Softball">softball</a> club in <a href="/wiki/Denmark" title="Denmark">Denmark</a>, founded in 1977. Over the years the Hurricanes have won 13 Danish Men's Championships and also provided the national team with numerous players. The Hurricanes have won the 2010, 2011 and 2012 Danish Championship, and the team also won the European Cup for European clubteams in 2011 and 2012. The Hurricanes play all <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Home_game" title="Home game">home games</a> at <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Hurricane_Park&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Hurricane Park (page does not exist)">Hurricane Park</a> in <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=H%C3%B8rsholm_Sportspark&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Hørsholm Sportspark (page does not exist)">Hørsholm Sportspark</a>.</p>
