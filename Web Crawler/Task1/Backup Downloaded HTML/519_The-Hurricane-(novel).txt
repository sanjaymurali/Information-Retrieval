<p><i><b>The Hurricane</b></i> is a 1936 novel by <a href="/wiki/Charles_Nordhoff" title="Charles Nordhoff">Charles Nordhoff</a> and <a href="/wiki/James_Norman_Hall" title="James Norman Hall">James Norman Hall</a> about a Pacific Ocean <a href="/wiki/Tropical_cyclone" title="Tropical cyclone">hurricane</a>. It was adapted into two films, <i><a href="/wiki/The_Hurricane_(1937_film)" title="The Hurricane (1937 film)">The Hurricane</a></i> (1937), directed by <a href="/wiki/John_Ford" title="John Ford">John Ford</a>, and <i><a href="/wiki/Hurricane_(1979_film)" title="Hurricane (1979 film)">Hurricane</a></i> (1979), by Swedish director <a href="/wiki/Jan_Troell" title="Jan Troell">Jan Troell</a>.</p>
