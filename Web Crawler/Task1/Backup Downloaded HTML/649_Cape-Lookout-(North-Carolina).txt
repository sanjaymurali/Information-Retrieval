<h1 class="firstHeading" id="firstHeading" lang="en">Cape Lookout (North Carolina)</h1>
<div class="mw-body-content" id="bodyContent">

<p><b>Cape Lookout</b> is the southern point of the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Core_Banks" title="Core Banks">Core Banks</a>, one of the natural <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Barrier_Island" title="Barrier Island">barrier islands</a> on the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Atlantic" title="Atlantic">Atlantic</a> coast of <a href="/wiki/North_Carolina" title="North Carolina">North Carolina</a>, <a href="/wiki/United_States" title="United States">USA</a>. It delimits <a href="/wiki/Onslow_Bay" title="Onslow Bay">Onslow Bay</a> to the west from <a href="/wiki/Raleigh_Bay" title="Raleigh Bay">Raleigh Bay</a> to the east. <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Core_Banks" title="Core Banks">Core Banks</a> and <a href="/wiki/Shackleford_Banks" title="Shackleford Banks">Shackleford Banks</a> have been designated as parts of the <a href="/wiki/Cape_Lookout_National_Seashore" title="Cape Lookout National Seashore">Cape Lookout National Seashore</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
<p>Cape Lookout is part of <a href="/wiki/Carteret_County,_North_Carolina" title="Carteret County, North Carolina">Carteret County</a> and is located 11 miles southeast of <a href="/wiki/Beaufort,_North_Carolina" title="Beaufort, North Carolina">Beaufort, NC</a>. Cape Lookout is one of North Carolina's three major <a href="/wiki/Cape_(geography)" title="Cape (geography)">capes</a>; the other two are <a href="/wiki/Cape_Hatteras" title="Cape Hatteras">Cape Hatteras</a> to the north and <a href="/wiki/Cape_Fear_(headland)" title="Cape Fear (headland)">Cape Fear</a> to the south.</p>
<p>The <a href="/wiki/Cape_Lookout_Lighthouse" title="Cape Lookout Lighthouse">Cape Lookout Lighthouse</a> is located on the cape, approximately three miles northeast of the point of the cape.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
