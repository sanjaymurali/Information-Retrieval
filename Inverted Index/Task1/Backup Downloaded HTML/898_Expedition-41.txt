<p><b>Expedition 41</b> was the 41st expedition to the <a href="/wiki/International_Space_Station" title="International Space Station">International Space Station</a>. It began on 10 September 2014 with the undocking of <a href="/wiki/Soyuz_TMA-12M" title="Soyuz TMA-12M">Soyuz TMA-12M</a>, returning the crew of <a href="/wiki/Expedition_40" title="Expedition 40">Expedition 40</a> to Earth.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
<p>The expedition ended with the undocking of <a href="/wiki/Soyuz_TMA-13M" title="Soyuz TMA-13M">Soyuz TMA-13M</a> on November 10, 2014. The remainder of Expedition 41's crew joined <a href="/wiki/Expedition_42" title="Expedition 42">Expedition 42</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
