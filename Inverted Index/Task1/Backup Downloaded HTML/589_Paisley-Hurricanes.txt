<p>The <b>Paisley Hurricanes</b> are an amateur <a href="/wiki/Rugby_league" title="Rugby league">rugby league</a> team. They play in the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Rugby_League_Conference_Scotland_Division" title="Rugby League Conference Scotland Division">Scotland Division</a> of the <a href="/wiki/Rugby_League_Conference" title="Rugby League Conference">Rugby League Conference</a>. The club is currently based at the <a class="external text" href="http://maps.google.co.uk/maps?q=Ross%20Street+Paisley+PA1%201TH" rel="nofollow">Anchor Recreation Grounds</a> in Paisley, home of Paisley Rugby Union Club.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Previous_Seasons">Previous Seasons</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Paisley_Hurricanes&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Previous Seasons">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><b>2007</b> After the relative success of the previous season, the Hurricanes finished third in the Scottish Conference losing to the Edinburgh Eagles in the Semi-Final at Union Park, Costorphine</p>
<p><b>2006</b> The Hurricanes overcame much difficulty in their first season in the Scottish Conference, but managed to fulfil all their fixtures and finish in a respectable fifth place in their inaugural season. In the process they recorded wins against Glasgow Bulls, Easterhouse Panthers and Moray Eels</p>
