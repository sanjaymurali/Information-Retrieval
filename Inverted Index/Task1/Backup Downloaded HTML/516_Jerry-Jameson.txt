<p><b>Jerry Jameson</b> (born November 26, 1934), is an American <a href="/wiki/Television_program" title="Television program">television</a> and <a href="/wiki/Film_director" title="Film director">film director</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Film_editor" title="Film editor">editor</a> and <a href="/wiki/Film_producer" title="Film producer">producer</a>. Highly prolific, he began career in the late 1960s as an associate producer and editorial supervisor on <a href="/wiki/The_Andy_Griffith_Show" title="The Andy Griffith Show">The Andy Griffith Show</a>, before going on to direct many episodes of shows like <i><a href="/wiki/The_Six_Million_Dollar_Man" title="The Six Million Dollar Man">The Six Million Dollar Man</a>,</i> <i><a href="/wiki/Ironside_(1967_TV_series)" title="Ironside (1967 TV series)">Ironside</a>,</i> <i><a href="/wiki/Dallas_(1978_TV_series)" title="Dallas (1978 TV series)">Dallas</a>,</i> <i><a href="/wiki/Murder,_She_Wrote" title="Murder, She Wrote">Murder, She Wrote</a>,</i> and <i><a href="/wiki/Walker,_Texas_Ranger" title="Walker, Texas Ranger">Walker, Texas Ranger</a>.</i> He also directed numerous made-for-TV movies and theatrical motion pictures, including <i><a href="/wiki/Airport_%2777" title="Airport '77">Airport '77</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/Raise_the_Titanic_(film)" title="Raise the Titanic (film)">Raise the Titanic</a></i>, and <i><a href="/wiki/Captive_(2015_film)" title="Captive (2015 film)">Captive</a>.</i></p>
