<p><i><b>Tropical Depression</b></i> is a four-song EP released by <a href="/wiki/Elephant_Micah" title="Elephant Micah">Elephant Micah</a> in February 2006 for his spring mini-tour. The CD was a limited edition of 100 (now sold out) that came in a numbered, potato-stamped sleeve.</p>
